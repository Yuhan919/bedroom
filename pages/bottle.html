The brush-washing cup holds water from several days ago,
with pale gray paint settled at the bottom, like something forgotten.
A thin layer of oil-like sheen floats on the surface, mingling with scattered reflections.

but I always feel that true emotion hides in this cup of water left aside—
time draws the colors closer and lets impulses settle into calm.
