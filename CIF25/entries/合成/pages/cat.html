cat

The Siamese kitten sits by the window, her ears twitching slightly as the hum of cars drifts from afar.
Her mother speaks of “university,” a distant place where humans go to learn and live.
But the kitten does not understand.
She only knows that her best friend will be gone for a very long time.
Moonlight spills across the floor as she curls up by the warm window.
Distance is too vast for her heart to measure.
Some say life begins with exploration,
but she feels that longing itself is a kind of learning— quietly shaping her days, wordless and tender.
